Your Shopping Cart

Your cart is where you’ll find all the items you’ve chosen to purchase. Review your selections, adjust quantities, and proceed to checkout with ease.

How to Manage Your Cart

  • View Items: See all products you’ve added to your cart.
  • Adjust Quantities: Update the quantity of items directly from your cart.
  • Remove Items: Delete items you no longer wish to purchase.
  • Proceed to Checkout: Complete your order and make a payment.

Cart Tips

  • Items in your cart are not reserved until you complete your purchase.
  • Check for discounts or promo codes before checking out.
  • Review your cart carefully to avoid missing any desired products.

Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve compiled answers to some of the most common questions our customers have. If you don’t find your question here, feel free to contact us.

1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and UPI payments for your convenience.

2. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can use this link to track the status of your order.

3. What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase. Please ensure that the product is unused and in its original packaging. Visit our Returns & Refunds page for more details.

4. Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, you can modify your delivery address within 24 hours of placing your order. Please contact our support team for assistance.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we deliver to selected countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location.

Still have questions?

If you need further assistance, visit the Contact Us tab or email us at support@ecommerce.com.
